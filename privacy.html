Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Meebo ("we", "us") operates the Meebo platform and website. This policy explains what we collect and how we use it. We keep it short and we keep it honest.

Information we collect

  • Information you give us. When you request access or contact us, we collect your name, email, company, and any message you send.
  • Product data. If you use Meebo, we store the molecular data, designs, and results you create in your workspace so the product can function.
  • Usage data. Standard technical logs (e.g. browser type, pages visited) to keep the service secure and reliable.

How we use it

  • To respond to access requests and provide the Meebo platform.
  • To operate, secure, and improve our products.
  • To send you product updates you have asked for. You can opt out at any time.

Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only with service providers that help us run the business (for example, form and email infrastructure), under appropriate agreements, or where required by law.

Your data, your control

You can request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information at any time by contacting us. We retain information only as long as needed to provide the service or as required by law.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Reach us through the contact form and we'll get back to you.
