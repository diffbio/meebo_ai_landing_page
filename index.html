§ 04 · Why Meebo

Predictions are cheap. Decisions are hard.

AI protein design is breaking open: generative models propose binders, enzymes, and therapeutics by the thousand. Every candidate needs structure prediction, property inference, and ranking before you know what to pursue.

But that inference lives in scattered notebooks, scripts, and clusters. Runs are hard to compare, provenance gets lost, and the analytics that should turn predictions into decisions never get built.

Meebo brings it together: run any model in one workspace, scale inference on demand, and layer analytics on top, so you can rank designs and act in minutes, not weeks.

And it compounds. Every run is captured, every comparison is reusable, and your analytics get sharper as your program grows.

Inference is only an answer when you can act on it.
